What can we do?

  • Voice-to-text

    Voice to text search functionality that allows a Owner/Customer to describe a product using their voice, then convert their speech into text, and finally take that text and search for items whose description is close to what they are looking for.

  • AI assistant

    AI assistant that notifies Owner/Consumer if a product is running low on stock and needs to be ordered, If a product has increased sales and if more should be ordered, if a product has decreased sales and suggest that an item be removed or a price change

  • Live Notifications

    When an item is purchased via a barcode or item ID, the website/app will automatically decrement the stock of the item by the number of the amount purchased of a specific item, and if an item is returned it will increment by 1
